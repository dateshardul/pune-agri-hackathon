## Strategic Assessment (from earlier analysis)
### Strong innovations:
- Dakaargala-AI (bio-indicator → soil mapping) — novel, defensible, buildable
- Electrostatic Dhoopana — real physics, novel application
- Mandala-grid UWB navigation — practical cost reduction
- Kunapajala 2.0 — amino acid bio-stimulants are legit

### Risky for technical jury:
- Nakshatra-AI / Astro-Meteorology — unvalidated science
- PEMF seed priming with honey/ghee — unfalsifiable without controlled trials
- Heavy "Vedic" branding — can seem pseudoscientific

### Recommended presentation framing:
Lead with engineering innovations. Vedic angle = story/differentiator, NOT the technical foundation.
